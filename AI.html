AI Introduction

Artificial intelligence (AI) is a technology that allows computers to perform tasks that usually require human intelligence. It can learn, solve problems, and make decisions. AI is used in many applications, including customer service, marketing, and transportation. How AI works AI systems receive data from humans, and then produce a recommendation based on algorithms. AI can recognize images, understand and respond to language, and learn from new information. AI can process large amounts of data quickly, which helps it find patterns and solve complex problems. Examples of AI Optical character recognition (OCR): Uses AI to extract text and data from images and documents Voice assistants: Like Siri and Alexa, which are based on AI technology Customer service chatbots: Which help you navigate websites Self-driving cars: Which can act independently, replacing the need for human intervention Benefits of AI AI can make businesses more efficient and profitable. It can also help humans by solving complex problems, making recommendations, and analyzing data.
