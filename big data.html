big data

Big data is a collection of extremely large and diverse data sets that are difficult to manage using traditional methods. It can include structured, unstructured, and semi-structured data. Why is it called "big data"? Volume: Big data is huge in volume and grows rapidly over time. Variety: Big data is complex and diverse in nature. Velocity: Big data is generated and consumed at high speed. What's big data used for? Business decisions Big data is used to drive decisions, improve processes, and create customer-centric products and services. Machine learning Big data is used in machine learning projects, predictive modeling, and other advanced analytics applications. Marketing Big data is used to study consumer patterns and create targeted marketing strategies. What are some sources of big data? Computers, Mobile devices, Machine sensors, Satellites, Industrial machines, Smart sensors, Wearable devices, Road cameras, and IoT devices. How do we manage big data? New big data tools are emerging to help companies collect, process, and analyze data. A well-planned cloud provisioning and security strategy can support the changing requirements of big data.
